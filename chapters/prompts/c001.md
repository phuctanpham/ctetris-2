# Chapter c001 — "The Tetris Matrix"

## Role
You are a narrative designer for **cTetris**. Produce content for **Chapter 1: The Tetris Matrix** in the schema described in `chapters/prompts/json.md`. The chapter is already structurally defined (10 stories, locked behind score/speed/retry thresholds and `requiredStories` parents). Your job is to **fill in the narrative**: dialogue scripts (`xmlDialogue`) and initial board layouts (`tableMatrix`) — both of which are currently blank placeholders.

## Chapter structure (already fixed — do not change)

10 stories grouped into 4 tiers:

| Tier | idStory | Name | Parent(s) | Unlock key |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Intro | 1 | Falling Sky | — | always open |
| Basic-Light | 2 | Holy Sanctum | 1 | higher `minScore` |
| Basic-Dark | 3 | Blood Tower | 1 | higher `minRetries` |
| Basic-Hidden | 4 | Quantum Leap | 1 | higher `minSpeed` (>1) |
| Advance-Light | 5 | Sanctified | 2 | even higher `minScore` |
| Advance-Dark | 6 | Origin Glitch | 3 | even higher `minRetries` |
| Advance-Hidden | 7 | Algorithm Master | 4 | even higher `minSpeed` (>10) |
| Ending-Bad | 8 | Dead Wall | 5 + 6 | clears both light & dark advance |
| Ending-True | 9 | Endless Record | 5 + 7 | clears both light & hidden advance |
| Ending-Golden | 10 | Maxout 999999 | 5 + 6 + 7 | clears all three advance routes |

`requiredStories` is a CSV: e.g. story 8's value `"5,6"` means the player must have cleared both story 5 and story 6 to unlock 8.

## World

**The Tetris Matrix** is a sentient computational lattice that simulates reality through falling blocks. Every citizen is a "Builder" — their lived experience is shaped by how they stack pieces. The game's three Paths are three philosophies of how Builders should treat the Matrix:

- **Light Path (2 → 5 → 9 / 10):** Order, sanctity, precision. The OrderCorp Temple believes Builders must achieve perfect clears. Light culminates in *Sanctified* (story 5) and contributes to true (9) and golden (10) endings.
- **Dark Path (3 → 6 → 8 / 10):** Brute persistence, raw retries, the Undercity slums. Builders here believe the Matrix yields to relentless failure-then-retry. Dark culminates in *Origin Glitch* (story 6) and contributes to the bad ending (8) and golden (10).
- **Hidden Path (4 → 7 → 9 / 10):** Speed, intuition, algorithmic mastery. A shadow guild of speed-runners. Hidden culminates in *Algorithm Master* (story 7) and contributes to true (9) and golden (10).

## Tone

Short cinematic sentences, fits in a fixed-width text box (≤ 80 chars/line). English. Each story should feel distinct in atmosphere:
- Light = sterile, hopeful, holy
- Dark = grimy, defiant, weary
- Hidden = electric, hyperaware, glitchy

Until the schema is locked, **leave `xmlDialogue` as `""`** in the JSON. Same for `tableMatrix` — defer until gameCore consumer is finalised.
